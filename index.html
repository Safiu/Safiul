Dream

While I was doing my undergrad. study, I wasted a lot of time, I confess! When i learnt/realised that not doing anything creative makes my life as worthless as the garbage we throw away, I started searching for the perfect thing I could do for the world. I am not a person who solely thinks about his career and so I want to contribute to the people. I learnt that when I saw traditional curriculam doesn't always make a person capable of doing what s/he wants. I thought that it'd be worthy to establish an institution where anyone can learn what s/he wants. I want to build a library, an application, a lab where anyone can research on what they're interested in. Nothing else.. Because I believe if anyone knows what s/he wants to do with his/her life, they can definitely make the best out of it. My Dream Is To Create Such An Institution Where Everyone Will Learn, Test, Debug and Get Results From Whatever They Want To Do...
